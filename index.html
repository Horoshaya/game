History

Humanity has always been looking for extraterrestrial civilizations. But it turned out that they found us earlier... How to play?

  • Enter your name and press "Enter"

  • Choose task using the first sumbol of task name on your keyboard
  • Solve the task, write the answer and press "Enter"
  • Wait for resalt and continue play
  • If you want to see the result press "Esc"
